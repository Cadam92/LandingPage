What is Digital Innovation?

We identify and evaluate high-impact digital innovations to enable EST and the broader ATO to drive appropriate investment decisions (and avoid hype-driven, reactive mistakes).

Utilising internal and external research we capture technology-related opportunities within the wider enterprise.

Responding to increasing complexity and number of emerging technologies that EST needs to monitor for potential adoption, and supporting ATO Innovation with the development of the ATO Innovation strategy and articulating value of staff innovation ideas.

The opportunities for the ATO fall into four categories:

Client Experience , Disruptive Technology , IT Efficiences , Workforce Capabilities

Our Approach

Collect

Attract the right innovations

Attract digital innovations worth testing from internal entrepreneurs, vendors, partners and scanning the environment.

Test

Test, fail, validate the values

Rapidly refine, develop, test and fail or validate measurable value of digital innovations. Collaboratively work with clients, vendors, partners and internal stakeholders.

Invest

Make informed investment decisions

Pitch the digital innovation to an investment panel. Demonstrate the innovation, how it works, it's unique value and feasibility. Invest in ideas that provide the greatest measurable value.
